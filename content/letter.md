# To You 

Happy Birthday. 

Today this virtual museum is open only for you. 
Not to impress anyone, but to honor someone I truly admire. 

I admire your kindness, your strength, and the way you stay genuine. 
I admire how your smile can soften a long day. 
I admire how your presence makes ordinary moments feel meaningful. 

This place is my small way of saying: 
you are deeply appreciated, more than these words can hold. 

If time ever blurs some memories, 
I hope this museum keeps the feeling safe for us.
